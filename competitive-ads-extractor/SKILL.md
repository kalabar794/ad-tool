# Competitive Ads Extractor

Uncover what your competitors are saying, showing, and selling. This skill extracts ads from major ad libraries and delivers actionable competitive intelligence—messaging themes, creative patterns, CTA strategies, and gaps you can exploit.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning a new ad campaign and need inspiration
- Want to see what messaging competitors are testing
- Need to benchmark your creative against the competition
- Preparing a competitive analysis for stakeholders
- Looking for gaps in competitor advertising to exploit
- Monitoring competitor ad activity over time
- Building a swipe file of effective ad copy and creative

## What This Skill Does

1. **Extracts ads** from Meta, TikTok, Google, and LinkedIn ad libraries
2. **Captures ad copy** including headlines, body text, and CTAs
3. **Categorizes ads** by type (promotional, testimonial, educational, etc.)
4. **Analyzes messaging** to identify themes, keywords, and patterns
5. **Maps landing pages** to see where competitors drive traffic
6. **Generates reports** including executive summaries and gap analyses

## What You Can Learn

### Messaging Analysis
- Primary value propositions competitors lead with
- Pain points they're addressing
- Language and terminology they use
- Emotional triggers in their copy

### Creative Patterns
- Visual styles and formats that dominate
- Video vs static vs carousel mix
- Use of faces, products, or abstract imagery
- Color schemes and branding consistency

### Copy Formulas
- Headline structures that appear repeatedly
- CTA phrasing preferences
- Offer positioning (free trial, discount, demo)
- Social proof integration methods

### Campaign Strategy
- Platform allocation (where they spend most)
- Audience targeting signals from ad content
- Seasonal or promotional timing patterns
- A/B testing variations in market

## Best Practices

### Ethical Use
✓ Use insights for inspiration and strategy
✓ Create original ads informed by patterns
✓ Identify gaps to differentiate your brand
✓ Understand market positioning
✗ Never copy ad creative directly
✗ Don't plagiarize competitor copy
✗ Avoid using competitor trademarks

### Analysis Tips
1. Look for what competitors do consistently—it's likely working
2. Note what's missing—gaps are opportunities
3. Track changes over time to spot strategy shifts
4. Compare across platforms for channel-specific learnings
5. Focus on patterns, not individual ads

## Tips for Success

1. **Start broad, then narrow** — Extract everything first, then filter to what matters
2. **Look for consistency** — Ads that run repeatedly are likely performing well
3. **Mind the gaps** — What competitors don't do is often more valuable than what they do
4. **Track over time** — Monthly monitoring reveals strategy shifts
5. **Cross-reference platforms** — Different channels often show different strategies
6. **Focus on patterns** — One ad is an idea; ten similar ads is a strategy
7. **Build a swipe file** — Save the best examples for future inspiration
